Baking Since 2018

How Wake Cake Began

Wake Cake started in a tiny kitchen with one oven and a grand dream: to bring premium, elegant, and mouth-watering desserts back to the center of celebrations.

Over the years, we have grown into a high-end patisserie brand beloved by thousands. We focus on marrying classic French pastry techniques with modern design aesthetics, using only organic, locally-sourced ingredients to preserve the rich authenticity of every bite.
